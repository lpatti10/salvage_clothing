THE PROBLEM WITH DONATING

Many charity pick-up services and thrift stores are overwhelmed by the volume of donated clothing. Stained or damaged textiles can’t be resold and are typically disposed of. Synthetic fibers common today are problematic and hazardous to the decomposition process. We aim to take the unsellable clothes off the hands of these hard-working organizations.
